Privacy Policy

1. Introduction
EAD Property Management (“the Company”) is committed to protecting the privacy of its clients, tenants, and website users. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.
2. Information Collection
We may collect the following types of personal information:
  • Contact Information: Name, address, phone number, and email address.
  • Financial Information: Bank account details, payment history, and credit information.
  • Property Details: Lease agreements, maintenance requests, and occupancy status.
  • Website Usage: IP addresses, browser type, and pages accessed on our website.
    • 3. Use of Information
    The personal information collected is used for the following purposes:
  • Service Delivery: Managing properties, processing payments, and addressing inquiries.
  • Communication: Notifying clients about important updates, policy changes, or promotional offers.
  • Legal Compliance: Fulfilling obligations under applicable laws and regulations.
    • 4. Information Sharing
    We may share personal information with:
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors assisting in operations, such as maintenance contractors and payment processors.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect the Company's legal rights.
  • Affiliated Entities: Within our corporate family for internal administrative purposes.
    • 5. Data Security
    The Company implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
    6. Data Retention
    Personal information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.
    7. Individual Rights
    Individuals have the right to:
  • Access: Request a copy of their personal information held by the Company.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccuracies in their personal information.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of their personal information, subject to legal constraints.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of their personal information for certain purposes.
    • 8. Cookies and Tracking
    Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyze site traffic. Users can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings.
    9. Third-Party Links
    The Company's website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and encourage users to review their privacy policies.
    10. Policy Updates
    We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Clients will be notified of significant changes via the Company's official communication channels.
